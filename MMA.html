My Favorite MMA Fighters

Topuria Image

Ilia Topuria

Currently undefeated, Ilia Topuria is a Georgian-Spanish MMA fighter. He's 28 years old, and is the reigning featherweight champion of the UFC.

Current Record: 17-0-0

Recently knocked out Charles Oliveria, Max Hollway, and Alexander Volkanovski, who are three absolutely elite fighters. Before Ilia, Max Holloway was never knocked out in his entire pro career!

Pereira Image

Alex Pereira

Alex Pereira is a Brazilian MMA fighter, previously kickboxer primarily. He is 38 years old, and a previous Light Heavyweight and Middleweight champion of the UFC.

Current Record: 12-3-0

Recently knocked out Khalil Rountree Jr, Jiri Prochazka, and Jamahal Hill. Khalil Rountree is insanely good at fighting, and the fact that Alex Pereira knocked him out as if it was easy says something!

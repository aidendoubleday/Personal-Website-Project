Post High School Plans

Initial Plans:

My first plan is to enlist in the military when I graduate high school and do all four years that are required minimum. After I finish my four years in the military, I'll use my benefits to go to college for cheaper so I can study either law or engineering.

Once I finish college, I'll use my degree to find a job in whatever field I choose, and if I can't find a job in those fields, I'll apply to be an oil rig worker.

Plans After:

After I work for a couple of years, I'd look to start a family and just live out my life working and taking care of them.
